About Mark and Sons Projects

A legacy built by hand, project by project.

Founded by master carpenter Frank Makonese, Mark and Sons Projects has spent over 15 years shaping the homes, schools, offices and commercial spaces of South Africa with refined timber craftsmanship.

Every cut, joint and finish carries Frank's lifelong passion for woodworking and his unwavering commitment to the people we serve. We work shoulder-to-shoulder with our clients — from concept sketch to final install — to deliver pieces designed to outlast trends and generations.

Today, our skilled team operates across all nine provinces, serving homeowners, property developers, schools, government projects and businesses with the same customer-first approach that started it all.
